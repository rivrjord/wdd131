Dream Nigeria — Site Plan

Showcasing the Beauty, Culture, and Spirit of Nigeria

Purpose

The purpose of Dream Nigeria is to inspire pride in Nigeria’s heritage and promote tourism by highlighting destinations, cultural events, and travel opportunities. The site will engage users with dynamic features and responsive design.

Target Audience

  • Local and international tourists seeking authentic experiences.
  • Students and researchers exploring Nigerian culture.
  • Travel enthusiasts looking for destinations and tips.

Pages

  1. Home
  2. Destinations
  3. Culture & Festivals
  4. Plan Your Trip (form)
  5. Contact
  6. References

Features

  • Responsive design for all devices.
  • Dynamic navigation and interactive UI with JavaScript.
  • Lazy-loaded, optimized images for performance.
  • Interactive “Plan Your Trip” form saved in localStorage.
  • Content-rich sections with text, photos, and cultural highlights.

Scenarios

  • Fatima from Spain visits Dream Nigeria to discover top destinations and festivals. She uses the Plan Your Trip form to save her travel preferences.

  • Chinedu, a student in Lagos, explores the Culture & Festivals page for research on Calabar Carnival and checks the References page for sources.

  • Ada, living in the U.S., browses Dream Nigeria to reconnect with her roots, view destinations, and plan a future trip.
