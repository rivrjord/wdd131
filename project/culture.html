The Yoruba People

Known for their deep-rooted monarchy (Obas), intricate carvings, and vast traditional markets. The Yoruba culture is prominent in the South West, including Lagos and Ibadan.

The Igbo Nation

A highly decentralized and entrepreneurial people from the South East, known for their elaborate masquerades, Nri kingdom history, and Ozo titles system.
